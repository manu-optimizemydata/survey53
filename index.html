The Estate

Grounded in Devanahalli's countryside

Survey 53 sits on acres of farmland and woodland in Devanahalli — a place where stone paths, native planting, and warm timber interiors feel at one with the land around them.

The property is hired exclusively for your group. Whether you are hosting a leadership offsite or a family milestone, you have the full estate — gardens, pavilion, lakefront, and all — entirely to yourselves.

  • Corporate offsites and leadership retreats in a natural setting
  • Private celebrations — birthdays, anniversaries, engagements
  • Intimate pre-wedding functions by the water
  • Weekend wellness stays for small groups
